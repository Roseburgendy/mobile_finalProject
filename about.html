Who We Are?

POPPY Tokyo is a creative collective rooted in Harajuku's vibrant street style. We draw inspiration from Tokyo's colorful youth culture and reimagine it through bold designs and refined tailoring.

Our design philosophy centers on "Creativity Meets Craftsmanship": each garment blends playful aesthetics with high-quality materials, celebrating everyday self-expression.

Our team comprises Tokyo-based designers, visual artists, and skilled artisans who collaborate closely to bring POPPY's vision to life. We also engage with our community on Instagram @poppy_tokyo, sharing behind-the-scenes stories and new collection updates.

Every piece is designed to make you feel connected—to the city's pulse and your own creative spirit.

POPPY Tokyo — Street-inspired. Creative-led. Community-rooted.
